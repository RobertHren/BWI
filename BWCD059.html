BWCD059

Tektight

Evolution EP

Tektight delight us with nothing else than a fantastic three track ep, completely full of old school grooves that can be recognized from the Roland TR series. Evolution, which carries out the name of this ep, plays out with the rim shots and the fxs applied to them to make their way through the synth stabs and melody, leading the whole groove of the track beautifully. Order Disorder is just that, a great attempt to mess with your head, with similar base that we hear on Evolution but on this one, completely filled the spaces with apparently dissonant stabs, which are really none when you hear them as whole. Swing State makes you dance with this groovy bass line and simple base arrangements. But the main element here are the rare articulation created with the occasional synth sounds, which are also completely wet in subtle delay and pitch fxs during the break and the rest of the track. A delightful piece from the first sound to the last.
