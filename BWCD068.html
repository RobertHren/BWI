Christmas Sampler

Getting towards the end of a fantastic year we have the opportunity to celebrate Christmas at large. We are very proud of present this fantastic compilation including tracks from the very Basswalk man itself, Mr. Toby Holguin with his track Inri, and also with a new bunch producers that are making their own way on the label with a great collection of superb tracks. Some of the names are Samu Rodriguez, Simone Fonte, Castiblanco, Oxy Beat, Ektor Eros and Beartrax. We present this compilation including some amazing deep tech grooves that goes evolving softly track to track into serious tech sounds, ending up in two ground shaking techno tracks. We believe is a great way to celebrate this holydays and we are very excited to share this with you all. There is definitely some solid stuff in this compilation that will make dance the crowd in any dance floor for this Christmas.
