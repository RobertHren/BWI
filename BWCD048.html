BWCD048

Dubman F.

Moonlight EP

For this release, the Spanish producer, Dubman F. brought us a really well executed deep house track, with soft vocal touches, groovy bass line and synth stabs that complements perfectly with the pads at the background. One of the two great remixes is by GruuvElement's, who delivered an extremely groovy tech remix with a powerful low bass line, without leaving behind at any moment the original's vibe. The second and last remix is from the producer duo, P. Lopez & Alex AQ, from Paraguay, who decided to give this ep a funky touch with a catchy bass line and acid synths.
