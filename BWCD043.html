BWCD043

Francesco Piccirillo

Drafts EP

Great bass lines, old school synths and melodies and catchy rhythms are what Francesco Piccirillo brings us with this great two-track ep. Also two great remixes complete this ep, one by the hand of the Argentinean producer Omar Salgado and the second by the Italian A++. The first one with a great hypnotic vibe created by a perfectly executed synth line and the second with a powerful tech house groove specially made for the dance floor. A great ep to listen and play!
