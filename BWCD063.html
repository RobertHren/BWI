BWCD063

Angel Calderon, Kevin Sunset

Psicosis EP

We welcome this time to Angel Calderon & Kevin Sunset, who brought us a fantastic single with their Psicosis track. A powerful bass line with elaborated percussion rhythms, guide this track with an incredible simplicity into a very effective and complex work made by these two artists. Many fxs sounds, arrangements, delays and different vocals can be heard that truly complement it, giving the final tech house cherry to this truly mainstream track. Enzo Tucci decided to work with vocals and electric piano sounds as the main element present in his remix giving it a soft deep touch. All this mixed with the groovy percussion line and a funky bass line creates a beautiful variant for this ep. Mennie decided to go a bit further with a Nu Disco approach over his remix, introducing several synth lines and a super effective base and bass, that in combination with the original track elements, creates a beautiful vibe that worth every beat to hear.
