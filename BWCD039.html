Fabio Ferro & Max Noize

That Drop! EP

The Italian duo Fabio Ferro & Max Noize delivered an astonishing track, That Drop. A super pumpy tech house track with a strong bass line and powerful soul. The Spanish Necaa is the one in charge of the first remix which haves a great aura and sense including vocals and a fantastic atmosphere. Another great soft tech house track for the ep. For last Giuseppe Rizzuto is the man that gave the ep a different approach with a tech house remix but this time extremely groovy and dance floor rocking. You should check it!
