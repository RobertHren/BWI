BWCD028

PD Roke & Necaa

Aqua EP

A deep house masterpiece by the Spanish star producers Necaa and PD Roke. This ep has a great work at the vocals and housy pianos alongside a perfect base groove with strong bass lines. Very modern and cool sounds. The Luca Doobie Remix has a strong tech house rework and an awesome soft and delicated work with the vocals creating a remix with no precedents. Jerson Medina's Remix took the vocals with a soft delay and place then in another level creating a very groovy yet still deep variation from Aqua, with a great involving break. Perfect ep for the summer.
