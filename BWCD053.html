Summer Sampler 2013

To celebrate this fantastic summer, we have a complete compilation of seven clubly tracks, featuring artists like White Brothers, Riky Ild, Ektor Eros, Blaqwell, Di Chiara Brother's and Julian Garces. In this compilation we present mind blowing hypno tech house tracks like What Planet Is This, some serious tech house stuff like Toronto with a really catchy bass line, some really groovy and percussive tracks like Got Soul and The Ideal Replublic, an aggressive deep house jewel like Arrival 2008 and two deep techno bombs with Make Me King versions. We can say we have a bit of everything to every dj and music love to blow up any dance floor.
