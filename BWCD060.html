Mad_Us

She Dances In The Corner EP

Mad_Us duo came back but this time with the She Dances In The Corner EP, including two originals and two remixes. Wake Up presents a simple but strong groove created from a clean base, powerful tech bass, perfectly added synths and vocals at the background mixed in such beautiful way that already captured the attention of djs like Richie Hawtin who played it several times already. She Dances In The Corner has a more underground approach and also much aimed to the dance floor with a loopy and hypnotic base conformed by great percussions and a perfectly found bass line that complements the groove exquisitely. A great synth pad at the backend, helps finish and build what we can say a very refined track. Gianluca Caldarelli from Expenctancy Records, remixed She Dances In The Corner with a nice tech approach, that added this ep a gently touch of percussions and party vibe. A greatly articulated groove is present in this track, which has been already the choice of many artists like Marco Carola to rock many crowds. Luca Doobie worked around Wake Up, giving it a peculiar techno soul with simple and very effective filtered background fxs, complements perfectly this fantastic elements with a nice and minimalistic melody created from the original track synth line. A Truly fascinating ep in where every track presents a strong option to choose at the time of djing.
