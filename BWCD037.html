Basswalk Best Of 2012

Closing this amazing refreshing year has not been an easy work, but surely was a year full of emotion, great music, new trends and plenty of great experiences, but mainly we are really proud to get the chance to work with each and every artist from our roster. We are really happy to present this final compilation for this great 2012, including what we believe was the best of the best in Basswalk, featuring artists like Stefano Noferini, Pig & Dan, Ukka, Ronan Portela, Just2, Urss, Paolo Cocco, among others. Many of these tracks have been widely supported by top-tier djs during the course of 2012, but now you have a chance to re-check them before is too late. Happy new year!
