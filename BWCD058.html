BWCD058

Archila

Infection Groove EP

On this release we have the Colombian producer Archila, who bring us two great tracks with his Infection Groove EP. The first one, Infection, presents a killer bass line arranged in a classic techno way, alongside a repeated vocal line that evolves softly through the track, helping on the transitions and also creating a very nice mood on it. Single Rose Groove has a deeper soul on it with its soft tech groove and house synths in the back-end that are also setting the perfect base for the vocal line to appear in the right moments. The Spanish Lluis Ribalta, prepared his own remix of Infection, which also shows a tech house approach, but this one with a much more club vibe on it. A simple work with the vocals and bass line makes this track exceptionally effective in the dance floor, which will blow up after its explosive break. The Greek producer Nonyas, created powerful techno remix of Infection, completely full of base sounds and with a great groove elaborated in the low-end as well. All we can say about it, is that has trippy vibe that will blow your mind in the hard way.
