BWCD044

Giuseppe Rizzuto

Fear Developes EP

Giuseppe Rizzuto delivered a powerful two track ep full of fear! Amazing grooves, great vocal work, hypnotic percussions, strong-deep bass lines and an awesome underground vibe is what makes this ep the right choice to rock the dance floor. With it we welcome to a new artist to Basswalk, He Did from Lima, Peru. He made a really deep and cool remix of Fear Of Noise, with an astonishing and involving ambience, base rhythms and perfectly executed vocals. Not a sound wasted in this ep!
