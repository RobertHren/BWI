Hologram (Italy)

Yuppee EP

We give a big welcome to our new artist Hologram who debuts in Basswalk with this three track EP. Yuppee has a long and deep bass line with a really soft and nice vocal line that gives this track a really good party vibe. Besides that, an amazing intro, which is very useful to open main sets with a top-notch track. Fatal Headquake shows a soft techno soul mixed with some deep house elements in a very interesting, yet very powerful track. Works If You Are Done haves a more melodic way of presenting it's elements, like the bass and synth lines, making it a perfect closure for this great pack of tracks. Last and not least, we have the Andrea Colina, Victor Elle, Marco Capizzi Remix of Yuppee, which haves nothing else than groove all over it. A great bass line and subtle work with the vocals gives this amazing ambience and vibe, which combines perfectly with the low-end percussions, creating together the perfect base for this really strong back after the soft uplifting break. A must have ep for sure!
