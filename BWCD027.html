Angel Calderon

New York EP

By the hand of Angel Calderon & Viktor M, we have this incredibly groovy track, New York! With remixes from the Keep On Records label managers Quantizers, the Spanish duo ThreeJacks and the well know Bastian Pieper from Germany, complete this ep with super strong tech house re-interpretations, that each one will fit everyones taste when choosing tracks to rock the dance floor.
