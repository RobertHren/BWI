BWCD031

Paolo Cocco, Franco Baldaccini

Never EP

This time we give you an impressive work from the Italian producers Paolo Cocco and Franco Baldaccini. They delivered a great tech house track with a super work on the vocals and percussions. The Spanish Dubman F made an ellaborated re interpretation with an underground touch, yet super groovy and perfect for the dance floor. This time also with the amazing Playane has teamed up with Fabio Ferro & Max Noize duo to create a cleaner version of Never with an old school base and feeling a bit different from the original, but always having it soul present. All three amazing works that worth to listen!
