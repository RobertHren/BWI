BWCD035

Fabio Ferro

Around The Corner EP

Fabio Ferro gave a fantastic tech house track with a very simple and super effective soul, one of those tracks that blow up the dance floor in a great way. Another Italian artists, Mauro Alpha & Dalbe, remixed this great track creating a cool groove with original pieces, keeping its soul with an impressively modern way and with great and strong bass line as well. The last and not least remix was made by the Argentinean duo Nexus DJ & Mon, who reworked this track in a more melodic way towards a superb rhythmic bass line, creating a great atmosphere and groove. Really a must have a ep for everybody, djs and music lovers!
