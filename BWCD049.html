Ukka - Broken Phone EP

Another great release by Ukka, the Argentinean producer, who delivered a really great underground track. Broken Phone has the cool underground vibe that Ukka has us used to, with nice percussions, evolving pitched down fxs, great ambiences and a groovy bass line, all mixed perfectly with a great hand and in a super dynamic way. One of the remixes is from the Italian duo, Proudly People, and they definitely made a remix to be proud of! A simpler but strong base, with a deeper bass line is what defines this remix, which plays around with subtle reverbs and ambience stabs synths. The last remix has much more percussive approach, directly aimed to the dance floor's peak time moment, with some nice battery fills, catchy bass line and nice work with the dark vocals, Matt Keyl created the perfect remix for any club!
