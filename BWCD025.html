BWCD025

Alberto Tolo & Matthew Skud

Taco Girl EP

A great piece by the Italian duo Alberto Tolo & Matthew Skud. Organic sounds and tech house grooves is what makes this ep a great choice for playing during the nights. Ukka remix is a perfect selection of sounds and groove, apparently soft but with a super strong aura and feeling as he knows to create. Paolo Cocco remix is a super cool tech house re interpretation created with a remarkable subtleness. Groovy bass line and delicate work with the vocals alongside a great base work. All you need from an ep!
