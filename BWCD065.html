BWCD065

Dani Holl, Genny Effe

There You Again EP

Another Italian duo is making its stand at Basswalk, this time are Danny Holl & Genny Effe with their single There You Again. An impressive track that is full of tech house, a super groovy percussion line and a rock solid break that will blow any dance floor. That is what you will feel with this amazing track. Another two fantastic artists have remixed this single, A++ and Stefano Kosa. The first one has created a much techno re interpretation, but at the same time with a deep ambience that is also adding a great hypnotic sense to this track. Also the simple, but groovy base and bass lines, helps to create this ambience and drive you through this mind blowing remix. The second remix, by Stefano Kosa, translates this nice tech house vibe from the original into a fantastic bomb, full of energy and explosion everywhere. A great work with the vocals puts the cherry to this remix with a really subtle touch, which also complements perfectly with an occasional synth line creating a nice articulation. But the heart of this track resides in the solid percussions and the really strong bass line that can make the dead dance when it comes back after the long and uplifting break.
