Got Ya! EP

This time we have a special delivery from the Spanish producer Bertech, who gave us this two track ep full of groove and tech house feeling. Strong bass lines and great base lines make this ep a fantastic tool for the dance floor. Greenlips, from Italy, are the producers in hand of this groovy tech remix that really shakes the ground!
