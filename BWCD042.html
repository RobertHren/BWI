BWCD042

Brothers in Progress & Ivan Masa

Dirty Little Secret EP

Brothers In Progress & Ivan Masa! These guys have created a really cool ep with Mananero and Dirty Little Secret. Both tracks have plenty of work and you can hear it! From a groovy bass line, going through a subtle and articulated vocal work, to a powerful base with the addition of some old school synths lines, you get nothing but a great ep. And if that isn't enough we also included some amazing remixes from some great and well know producers like Ukka, who has delivered some great tracks in the past, as well as from London FM and Khaan. All tracks have their own soul and moment in the night, check them!
