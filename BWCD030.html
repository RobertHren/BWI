Carlos Hdez & Dubtechie

Loco Ritmo EP

This ep from Carlos Hdez and Dubtechie, is a real piece of work. From El Rolando, with its underground and dark tech groove, going through to Loco Ritmo, the ensign track of the ep, with its super groovy, funky and hypnotic atmosphere and great bass line, to the pure tech house re interpretation from ThreeJacks of Loco Ritmo, this ep has it all. You have a taste of every style and each one better than the previous one. Definitely you should check this release because it has no waste at all!
