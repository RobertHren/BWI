BWCD046

Maxikool

Next Level EP

Maxikool, a new comer to Basswalk who we receive with our arms wide open. This young Colombian producer delivered a solid two track ep. The ensign track Next Level shows us a powerful tech house style with a groovy bass line and strong percussions, while Che Chere, grows slowly with an astonishing bass line and hypnotic structure and sounds. These tracks are specially made for the club. We also have a perfectly executed deep tech remix from A++, who prepared nothing else than a master piece again. Great ambience in perfect balance with strong bass lines is what makes this ep a really good choice to use in any dance floor.
