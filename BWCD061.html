Alejandro Fernandez

Silver EP

Alejandro Fernandez has come large with his Silver EP. A massive pack of three original tracks designed to blow up your mind from every corner. Silver has a great sonic presence, completely defined by a robust base and a deep and powerful bass line. Occasional fxs and stabs create a wonderful underground vibe. Good Times plays around with a funny, yet very hypnotic, rhythm which resulted very effective for any dance floor. Cous marks its way with a great combination of vocal fxs, ambiences and soft backend percussions that sets the ground for a very groovy bass line. Juan Zolbaran also makes its entrance to Basswalk with a fantastic remix of Cous that presents a soft, and very well found, change in the groove with the audible hard work in the low-end. The soft percussions which complement the bass line, are just perfect to lead this track into a very cool atmosphere that also gets complemented by several fxs and reversed vocal sounds. A complete and very techy ep that will make its stand on the dance floors.
