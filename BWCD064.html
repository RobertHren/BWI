Danny O

How To Groove EP

We are very happy to welcome our family to Danny O, who brought us a rock solid two track EP. How To Groove shows exactly what its name says, a great combination of sounds with a groovy driving base and bass lines, that complements perfectly with the vocals and ambience sounds. Look To The Sky plays around with a much hypnotic and harmonic atmosphere created from an apparently simple but complex rhythmic groove that relies mostly on a solid percussion line and strong deep bass. These are the foundations for the Asian strings that gives the perfect finish for this track with a very cool touch. Joaco Cabrin from Buenos Aires, is the man in charge of remixing Look To The Sky and he made it with a superb deep tech bass and crazy arrangements in the background. All this mixed with the original strings helps create a beautiful vibe that will articulate softly getting you a great party mood.
