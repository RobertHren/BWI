BWCD040

Diego Infanzon

Attention EP

Diego infanzon bring us a super powerful original track, Your Attention, that has a rock solid base arrangements and amazing bass line. This ep also includes remixes by the great Spanish producer Frink, who did a really cool and deep remix with a very groovy bass line and a great work in the vocals. Pablo Rez did another remix with a more representative touch towards the original, but with some house notes that gives it a special vibe. A really great work from all the artists!
