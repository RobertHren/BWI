Skyline EP

This is JUST2 time with a Skyline. A great original track with a great tech house base and groovy percussions makes this track special for dance floor. Urss and Kike Mayor are the ones in charge the first remix, a really fine deep house interpretation. The Venezuelan producer Fiuset gave us house remix with a detailed work in the percussions and bass lines. A true must have ep!
