BWCD045

Luca Donzelli

Tiquismiquis EP

We are more than happy to welcome Luca Donzelli to Basswalk with his fantastic Tiquismiquis EP. His track has a mixture of old school groove and sounds, but with a really actual vibe created from a funky and catchy rhythm with a perfectly made melody by this retro synth line. A big pack of fxs and occasional sounds finish this track with the variations needed to keep you on top all the way. Another big addition to this ep is Sascha Sonido's remix, who delivered a groovy percussion re-interpretation but without losing the original essence for a minute. The second remix comes by the hand of the new collaboration from Argentina, Re Us, who gave a different twist to the ep with their groovy club remix. Big ambience and powerful bass lines defines this remix which also haves great attention to vocals and synths. A must have ep for any dj and music lover!
