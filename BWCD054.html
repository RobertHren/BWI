BWCD054

iHaus

Get Burned EP

We give a huge welcome to the Argentinean artist iHaus with his wonderful Get Burned EP; full deep, tech and very cool sounds. Get Burned has a subtle tech house vibe with many strong nu disco elements like the bass line. The underground vocals give to this track a delicate yet needed touch, which completely closes the idea of a simple but very strong track. Goty's has many similar elements with Get Burned, yet these arranged in different ways. A strong string bass, occasional fills and perfectly executed work in the vocal line, makes this track an exceptional piece of tech house perfect for any dance floor. Grey, with his Urss re-interpretation, presents a slightly different approach to the ep with a synthetic and super groovy bass line. Also the other main elements like the electric piano and synth stabs, present in the background and brought to us in a minimalistic way, alongside the perfect placement of fxs and short vocals, makes this track very delightful to our ears and of course, to play it. The last track from this ep, K17, is the one with more deep house elements on it. Synth stabs, simple base and string bass, are the pieces that put together this beautiful track from beginning to end, completing this amazing ep.
